What happens without KartaFlow

The average TRC rejection adds 3–6 months to your wait.

A rejection means reapplying from scratch, paying office fees again, and staying in legal limbo — sometimes losing your right to work while you wait. Most rejections are caused by document errors that a lawyer review would have caught in 24 hours.

Wrong document version — the most common rejection reason. The Voivodeship won't tell you which one until after.
Missing employer stamp — technically optional in some cases, required in others. Unless you know which, assume required.
Expired or soon-expiring documents — accepted at intake, flagged at review. Application void.
